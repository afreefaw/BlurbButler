April trip - costa rica

April 15 - Fly to San Jose
Meet up with my friend, Maria, at the airport
Stay in San Jose for a night or two
Visit the National Theater and the Pre-Columbian Gold Museum
April 18 - Take a bus to Manuel Antonio
Spend a few days at the beach
Go on a nature walk in the Manuel Antonio National Park
April 21 - Take a bus to Monteverde
Stay in a cabin in the cloud forest
Go ziplining or horseback riding
April 24 - Take a bus to La Fortuna
Visit the Arenal Volcano and the hot springs
Go on a hike in the Arenal National Park
April 27 - Take a bus back to San Jose
Spend a night or two in the city
Fly back home on April 29
